    mission

    KYMA exists to provide mentorship, guidance, and opportunities for youth and students to grow in character, leadership, innovation, and career readiness through structured programs, partnerships, and community engagement.

    vision

    To inspire and empower Kenyan youth and students to become confident, ethical, and visionary leaders who positively shape their communities and the nation.

    objectives

    KYMA shall pursue the following objectives: 1. Empower Kenyan youth with mentorship and leadership skills. 2. Build strong partnerships with schools, universities, and industries. 3. Promote values, character development, and national identity. 4. Support youth in entrepreneurship, innovation, and career readiness. 5. Foster community service, spiritual growth, and social responsibility. .

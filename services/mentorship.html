MENTORSHIP, GUIDANCE, AND COUNSELING DEPARTMENT

Inspiring growth, guiding futures, and transforming lives through mentorship and moral guidance.

Program Director

Description:

This department designs mentorship frameworks for schools, colleges, and communities. It trains mentors, offers counseling sessions, and promotes moral integrity and responsible living among members through continuous follow-up and support.

Our Objectives:

  1. Develop mentorship frameworks for schools, colleges, and communities.
  2. Train mentors and counselors under KYMA standards.
  3. Offer counseling sessions to youth facing academic or personal challenges.
  4. Promote moral integrity and responsible living among members.
  5. Collaborate with faith-based and educational institutions for youth programs.
  6. Design mentorship materials and handbooks.
  7. Monitor mentees’ progress and provide follow-up support.

LEADERSHIP AND TRAINING DEPARTMENT

Welcome to the leaadership and training department where we lead, train, track and empower


program director

Description:

This department develops and empowers young leaders through training, workshops, and leadership development programs. It focuses on building capacity, instilling servant leadership values, and fostering ethical and visionary leadership among KYMA members.

Our Objectives:

  1. Organize leadership and personal development training for KYMA members.
  2. Develop leadership curricula aligned with KYMA's vision and values.
  3. Identify and nurture emerging young leaders across counties.
  4. Facilitate workshops and capacity-building forums.
  5. Collaborate with external trainers and partners.
  6. Evaluate leadership programs' effectiveness.
  7. Promote servant leadership, excellence, and ethical conduct.
